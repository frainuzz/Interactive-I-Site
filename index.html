The whole duty of Typography, as of Calligraphy, is to communicate History to the
imagination, without loss by the way, the thought of image intended to be communicated by the Author. And the whole duty of beautiful typography is not to substitute for the beauty or interest of the thing thought Explore Type and intended to be conveyed by the symbol, a beauty or interest of its own, but, on the one hand, to win access for that communication by Resources the clearness & beauty of the vehicle, and on the other hand, to take advantage of every pause or stage in that communication to interpose some characteristic & restful beauty in its own art.
